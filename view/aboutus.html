ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We’re KnockOut Zone, and we’ve been bringing UFC fans closer to the sport since 2025.

Our website is about streaming UFC combat events, providing trending content and news for fans of the sport, and selling tickets for upcoming fights.

WHY WE EXIST

We love UFC, and we believe it would be easy and fun to watch fights, read news, and buy tickets online.

This is why we created this website to offer fans a place where they can enjoy everything about the sport.

WHAT WE DO

We would like to share UFC Combat events to make them more visible for everyone and make people like it, because sharing our passion into the UFC can be amazing.

We also want to improve the website by adding live fight updates, simple stats, a forum for fans to talk, a timeline with fighter history, fun quizzes, videos of the best moments, and an easy way to buy tickets.

OUR MISSION VISION VALUES

MISSION

Our goal is to bring UFC fans closer to the sport by streaming fights, sharing news, and selling tickets.

We want to create a fun and easy way for people to enjoy UFC.

VISION

We want to become the leading website for UFC fans, offering a platform where they can watch fights, learn about fighters, and talk with other fans.

Hoping to make UFC more popular and exciting for everyone.

VALUES

We provide UFC fans the ultimate experience. We offer a fun and friendly place to talk about fights.

We make it easy to watch UFC and buy tickets. Our team works hard to improve the site all the time.

We always share real, exciting, and up-to-date UFC content.
